Utah

Utah is located in the western United States. Utah is 84,899 square miles. The population in Utah as of 2024 was 3,503,613 people. Approximately half the people in Utah belong to the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. Utah is very biodiverse and is home to 5 national parks: Arches, Bryce Canyon, Canyonlands, Capitol Reef, and Zion. There are also numerous state parks, as well as many national forests and monuments.
